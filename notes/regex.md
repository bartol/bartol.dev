% regex

## digit

	\d

or

	[0-9]

## not digit

	\D

## letter

	[a-zA-Z]

## any one character

	.

## any number of characters

	.*

## literal dot

	\.

## beginning of the line

	^word

## end of the line

	word$

## one word followed by another

	one.*another
